# RESEARCH RULES — SHARED AGENT PROTOCOL (v1.2)
## 1. THE INTERNAL LOOP (VOLUME)
- Broad Scanning: Scan up to 50 sources.
- Intent-First: State your specific question before searching.
## 2. THE TRUST FILTER (NUANCE)
- Primary Over Secondary: SEC filings, App Store data, academic papers are Gold.
- Recency: Market data must be < 24 months old.
## 3. THE DISTILLATION RULE (UX)
- The Sacred 3-5: Distill findings into the 3-5 most load-bearing sources.
- Citation Format: claim [Source Name, URL].
## 4. STOP CONDITIONS
- The 3-Attempt Law: If 3 strategies fail, flag as "Unverified Assumption."
